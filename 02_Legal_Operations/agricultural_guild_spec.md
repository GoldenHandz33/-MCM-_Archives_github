# GoldenHandz Agriculture & Media LLC
## Companion Guild Specifications: Kalo & Soursop Core

### 1. Focused Layering Strategy
*   **Overstory Canopy & Medicine:** Soursop anchors the pocket, maximizing localized micro-climate shade, moisture retention, and yielding high-value medicinal fruit.
*   **The Sovereign Foundation:** Kalo planted strategically within the prepared rock pockets, utilizing accelerated organic inputs to build deep humus over the basalt.
*   **Support Biomass:** Local nitrogen-fixers (like Pigeon Pea) used strictly to break the stone and feed the primary Kalo and Soursop crops.

### 2. Physical Amendments Per Pocket
1.  **Base Layer:** Crushed local volcanic cinders mixed with raw organic matter for aerated drainage.
2.  **Core Compost:** Heavy localized organic inputs inoculated with micro-organisms to accelerate stone breakdown.
3.  **Top Layer:** Consistent, heavy applications of cut guinea grass mulch to seal moisture around the Kalo stalks and Soursop roots.
