# GoldenHands Series
## Audio Architecture & Sound Design Specifications

### 1. The Rhythmic Foundation (The Ground Layer)
*   **Tempo:** 82 BPM steady, locked into a natural heartbeat rhythm.
*   **Instrumentation:** Low-tuned traditional African djembe and Hawaiian pahu drums hitting only on the 1 and 3 beats to allow space for dialogue.
*   **Sub-Bass Matrix:** A continuous, warm sine-wave sub-bass holding the root frequency at 432Hz, driving under the narrative to build a sense of ancient gravity.

### 2. Scene-Specific Cues: Act I
*   **Scene 1 & 2 (The Soil Awakening):** Minimalist percussion. High-frequency ambient wind textures shifting into low-mid drum strikes as the volcanic stone is cleared.
*   **Scene 3 (Ancestral Anchors - The Griot's Protocol):** The percussion shifts to an overlapping polyrhythm when the sound system is activated. The sub-bass drops an octave lower to dominate the acoustic environment.
*   **Scene 4 (The Perimeter Enforcement):** Drums cut out abruptly when the agent approaches, leaving only the raw, vibrating sub-bass hum. Polyrhythms kick back in forcefully the moment the spoken-word protocol begins.
