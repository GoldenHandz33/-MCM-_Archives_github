# GoldenHandz Agriculture & Media LLC
## Residential Perimeter Companion Planting Layout

### Zone 1: Immediate House Perimeter Grid
*   **Overstory / Structural Layer:** Hibiscus variants (provides windbreak, partial shade, and vibrant organic biomass/mulch material).
*   **Mid-Story Fruit Layer:** 'Emerald' Blueberry & Miracle Fruit (positioned to benefit from filtered light and localized moisture retention).
*   **Ground Cover / Aromatic Layer:** Star Jasmine (acts as a living mulch, suppresses invasive weeds, and deters pests with strong aromatics).

### Soil & Substrate Management (Puna District Specifications)
*   **Substrate Adaptation:** Direct planting tailored to the porous, well-draining volcanic rock and local cinder profiles.
*   **Organic Inputs:** Local compost, wood chips for moisture management, and targeted acidic organic matter for the acid-loving blueberries.
